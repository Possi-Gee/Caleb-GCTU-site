INDUCTIVE REASONING

Inductive reasoning plays an important role of the Electronic Transaction levy (E-Levy). Policymakers likely draw conclusions based on insufficient premises, such as past economic trends or theoretical models, to justify implementation of the E-Levy. They might use data on the growth of digital transactions and potential revenue gains to support their decision. Again, the complexity of the economic system means theses premises can never fully capture relevant factors, making the conclusion uncertain. So, implementing the E-Levy involve making judgments based on incomplete information, which is characteristic of inductive reasoning.
